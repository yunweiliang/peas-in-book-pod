What/Who is a Reading Pea?

Are you stuck with Economist magazines, literary classics, or fantastic science fiction piled in your basement? Are you stuck in quarantine A Reading Pea is a advid reader who learned to appreciate the values, smell, and texture of physical books or someone who is stuck with too many collected over the years and want to share those knowledge!

Why not digital?

Digital books are great! They are good for the environment and easy to carry. But there are still existing books circulating in the world improperly recycled, crowding your shelf. There are also people not ready to give up physical books yet and buying many more, contributing a market demand on physical books still! (think portugal)
